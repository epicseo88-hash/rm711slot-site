Responsible Gambling

Slots are entertainment, not a way to make money. The moment they stop being fun, it is time to stop.

Set limits before you start

Decide a deposit amount you are comfortable losing, and treat it as the cost of an evening out. Never chase a loss by depositing more, and never play with money meant for rent, food or bills.

Warning signs

  • Playing longer or depositing more than you planned
  • Chasing losses to win back what you lost
  • Borrowing money or hiding your play from family
  • Feeling anxious or low when you are not playing

If any of these sound familiar, take a break and talk to someone.

Where to get help

Free, confidential support is available at BeGambleAware and Gambling Therapy. You can also ask live chat to set a deposit limit or self exclude your account.
